The ANTICIG Manifesto

Fashion is rebellion in fabric form

Born in the streets of Ludhiana and refined by the restless energy of modern India, ANTICIG exists for those who refuse to be ordinary. Every thread is chosen with intention. Every silhouette is designed to disturb the comfortable and comfort the disturbed.

We source premium cotton blends, handwoven khadi, and ethically produced fabrics — because standing out shouldn't come at someone else's expense.
